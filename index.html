Smart Waste Management

The Smart Waste Management Marketplace is a comprehensive solution designed to bridge the gap between sellers of recycled items or materials and potential buyers. This innovative platform provides an online marketplace where individuals and businesses can list recycled products for sale, and customers can browse, purchase, and benefit from sustainable products. Our website aims to promote recycling, reduce waste, and contribute to a circular economy by making it easier to buy and sell recycled goods.

Online Bus Ticket Booking Service

The Online Bus Seat Booking System is an intuitive and user-friendly web platform designed to facilitate bus ticket bookings between cities. Inspired by popular services like Redbus, this project enables users to easily search for bus routes, select seats, and make bookings. The system is integrated with a MySQL database to keep real-time track of seat availability and update the status of booked and unbooked seats dynamically.
